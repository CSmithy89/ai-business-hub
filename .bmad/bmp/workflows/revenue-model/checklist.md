# Revenue Model Validation Checklist

## Revenue Streams
- [ ] Primary revenue stream identified
- [ ] Secondary revenue streams considered
- [ ] Revenue timing clear (upfront/recurring/usage)
- [ ] All streams align with value delivery

## Model Selection
- [ ] Revenue model type selected
- [ ] Model fits business model canvas
- [ ] Model appropriate for customer segment
- [ ] Model sustainable long-term

## Expansion Revenue
- [ ] Upsell paths identified
- [ ] Cross-sell opportunities noted
- [ ] Net revenue retention target set
- [ ] Expansion strategy documented

## Financial Integration
- [ ] Revenue model connects to pricing strategy
- [ ] Revenue model feeds financial projections
- [ ] Unit economics aligned

## Risk Assessment
- [ ] Revenue concentration risks identified
- [ ] Customer dependency risks noted
- [ ] Market risks considered
- [ ] Mitigations documented
