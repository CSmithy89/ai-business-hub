# Financial Projections Validation Checklist

## Assumptions Documentation
- [ ] All revenue assumptions documented with rationale
- [ ] All cost assumptions documented with rationale
- [ ] All customer assumptions documented with rationale
- [ ] Confidence level assigned to each assumption
- [ ] Assumptions that need validation are flagged
- [ ] No hidden or unstated assumptions

## Industry Benchmarks
- [ ] Benchmarks cited for CAC
- [ ] Benchmarks cited for LTV
- [ ] Benchmarks cited for churn rate
- [ ] Benchmarks cited for gross margin
- [ ] Sources provided for all benchmarks
- [ ] Benchmarks appropriate to business model type

## Unit Economics
- [ ] CAC calculated correctly
- [ ] LTV calculated correctly
- [ ] LTV:CAC ratio calculated (target >3:1)
- [ ] Payback period calculated (target <12 months)
- [ ] Gross margin calculated
- [ ] Metrics compared to benchmarks
- [ ] Health assessment provided

## Revenue Projections
- [ ] Revenue model matches business model canvas
- [ ] Growth rates are justified
- [ ] Monthly projections for Year 1
- [ ] Quarterly projections for Years 2-3
- [ ] Annual projections for Years 4-5
- [ ] Revenue tied to customer count and ARPU

## Cost Projections
- [ ] Fixed costs itemized
- [ ] Variable costs per unit specified
- [ ] COGS calculated correctly
- [ ] Hiring plan reflected in costs
- [ ] Costs scale appropriately with growth
- [ ] Cost-driven vs value-driven approach clear

## Income Statement
- [ ] All line items calculated correctly
- [ ] Gross profit = Revenue - COGS
- [ ] EBITDA = Gross Profit - OpEx
- [ ] Net income calculated after D&A, interest, tax
- [ ] Margin percentages calculated
- [ ] Path to profitability shown

## Cash Flow
- [ ] Operating cash flow calculated
- [ ] Investing cash flow included
- [ ] Financing cash flow included
- [ ] Net cash flow correct
- [ ] Beginning and ending cash tracked
- [ ] Burn rate calculated
- [ ] Runway calculated

## Scenario Analysis
- [ ] Conservative scenario provided
- [ ] Realistic (baseline) scenario provided
- [ ] Optimistic scenario provided
- [ ] Scenario adjustments are reasonable
- [ ] Key metrics compared across scenarios
- [ ] Most likely scenario identified

## Funding Requirements
- [ ] Total funding needed calculated
- [ ] Use of funds specified
- [ ] Funding stages/timing suggested
- [ ] Post-funding runway calculated
- [ ] Target milestones for funding defined

## Sensitivity Analysis
- [ ] Key variables identified
- [ ] +/- 20% impact calculated
- [ ] Most sensitive variables highlighted
- [ ] Risk areas identified

## Anti-Hallucination Compliance
- [ ] No invented benchmark numbers
- [ ] No false precision (ranges used where uncertain)
- [ ] No optimism bias without conservative baseline
- [ ] All numbers traceable to assumptions
- [ ] Uncertainty acknowledged where appropriate
