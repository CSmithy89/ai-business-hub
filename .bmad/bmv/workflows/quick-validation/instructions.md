# Quick Validation Instructions

<critical>This is a RAPID 30-minute validation - do not over-engineer</critical>

<workflow>

<step n="1" goal="Capture Idea (5 min)">
<action>Get one-sentence problem and one-sentence solution</action>
<action>Identify target customer in 10 words or less</action>
</step>

<step n="2" goal="Competitive Quick Search (10 min)">
<action>5 Google searches for existing solutions</action>
<action>List top 3-5 competitors found</action>
<action>Note: Are they well-funded? Growing? Dominant?</action>
</step>

<step n="3" goal="Market Existence Check (5 min)">
<action>Are people paying for similar solutions?</action>
<action>Quick estimate: Is market big enough? (>$100M SAM)</action>
</step>

<step n="4" goal="Founder-Fit Quick Check (5 min)">
<action>Why are YOU the right person?</action>
<action>What's your unfair advantage?</action>
</step>

<step n="5" goal="Decision (5 min)">
<scoring>
Score 1-5 on each:
- Problem clarity
- Market existence
- Competition beatable
- Founder fit
- Timing

Total: X/25
- 20-25: Strong GO - proceed to full validation
- 15-19: Conditional - address concerns, then validate
- 10-14: Weak - consider pivoting
- <10: NO-GO - find a different idea
</scoring>
</step>

</workflow>
