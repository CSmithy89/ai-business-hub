# UGC Curation Workflow

## Purpose
Discover, curate, and share user-generated content with proper attribution and rights management.

## Context Variables
- `{{brand_mentions}}` - Mentions to review
- `{{content_type}}` - Type of UGC sought
- `{{platforms}}` - Source platforms
- `{{rights_status}}` - Permission tracking

## Prerequisites
- Social listening enabled
- UGC rights templates prepared
- Content library access
- Legal guidelines understood

## Rights Management

| Rights Level | Allows | Doesn't Allow |
|--------------|--------|---------------|
| Standard | Organic repost with credit | Ads, editing |
| Extended | Organic + paid, minor edits | Major modifications |
| Commercial | Full commercial use | Depends on agreement |

## UGC Best Practices

- Always get explicit permission
- Always credit the original creator
- Don't edit content without permission
- Respond to creators when they engage
- Build relationships with top UGC creators
- Track performance to optimize strategy

## Error Handling

| Error | Resolution |
|-------|------------|
| No response | Follow up at 5 days, archive at 14 |
| Permission denied | Thank them, remove from queue |
| Content deleted | Use archived version with permission |
| Creator unhappy | Remove immediately, apologize |
