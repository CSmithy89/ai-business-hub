Handling Price Objections

check_circle Verified â€¢ Updated 2 days ago

Talk Track

"I completely understand that budget is a major factor in your decision. However, when we looked at the ROI analysis together, we saw that our solution saves your team approx [X hours] per week. If we focus on the value of that time saved, the cost difference is often recovered within the first 3 months."

Key Steps

1

Acknowledge and Validate

Don't be defensive. Validate their concern about the price to build trust.

2

Isolate the Objection

Ask: "Is price the only thing holding us back from moving forward?"

3

Pivot to Value

Re-anchor the conversation on the problems you are solving, not the line item cost.
