What is the policy for remote work expense reimbursements? Specifically regarding home office setup.

Just now

smart_toy
Scribe AI Agent

According to the 2024 Employee Handbook, employees are eligible for a one-time reimbursement for home office setup.

  • Full-time employees can expense up to $500 for ergonomic equipment (chair, desk, monitor stand).
  • Peripheral electronics (keyboard, mouse, webcam) are covered under the IT Hardware budget, separate from this allowance.
  • Receipts must be submitted via Expensify within 30 days of purchase.

Note: Standing desks over $300 require prior manager approval.
